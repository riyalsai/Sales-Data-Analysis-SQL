# 📊 Key Insights from Sales Data Analysis

## 1. Regional Performance
- **West** region leads with highest sales
- **South** region has lowest sales volume
- **East** region has best profit margin

## 2. Category Analysis
- **Technology** = highest revenue category
- **Furniture** = lowest profit margin
- **Office Supplies** = highest order volume

## 3. Sales Trends
- Sales grow consistently year over year
- **Q4 (Oct-Dec)** is always the strongest quarter
- Dip observed in **January-February** every year

## 4. Customer Insights
- Top 10 customers contribute 15% of total revenue
- Average order value = $229

## 5. Business Recommendations
- Focus marketing on Technology products
- Investigate why Furniture has low profit
- Target Q4 with special promotions
